Davin Choo

Incoming postdoctoral fellow at Teamcore @ Harvard, under Milind Tambe

I am currently actively exploring opportunities to apply principled algorithmic techniques to solve real-world problems and create a positive social impact. During my Ph.D., my works primarily focused on designing and analyzing algorithms for learning causal and probabilistic models, as well as learning-augmented algorithms.

I am broadly interested in designing practical algorithms with theoretical guarantees, and analyzing existing heuristics by providing guarantees for them under real-world assumptions. I like learning new things and skills.

If you think you are working on a problem that I might find interesting, do not hesitate to reach out to me to chat about it!

About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student (started Aug 2021) happily (co-)supervised by Arnab Bhattacharyya and Seth Gilbert in the School of Computing (SoC) of National University of Singapore (NUS), under the AISG PhD Fellowship Programme. Between my undergraduate and Masters, I worked for a while as an applied research scientist at DSO National Laboratories on projects that lie in the intersection of A.I. and security.
